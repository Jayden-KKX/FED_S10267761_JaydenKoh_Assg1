Activities

Explore a variety of sports suited for you. Learn more about each sport below:

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport where players aim to score by shooting a ball through a hoop. It's great for improving cardiovascular health and coordination.

Badminton

Badminton is a racquet sport played with a shuttlecock. It's an exciting, fast-paced game that helps build agility, endurance, and hand-eye coordination.

Swimming

Swimming is an excellent full-body workout that improves cardiovascular health, flexibility, and strength. It is a low-impact activity, perfect for all fitness levels.
